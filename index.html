Hello, I am Chih Hsiao.

With a background of fine arts and interaction design, Chih has close to two decades of experience designing software applications across diverse platforms including web, television, social network, mobile, and desktop operating systems. Since the 2000s, he has been an art director, a web producer, a designer, and a UX manager in small and large technology companies in New York City and the San Francisco Bay Area. He is currently leading the creative development of design systems and products that focus on cloud and multi-device user experiences. He is a gamer, a passionate music collector and a jazz pianist.
