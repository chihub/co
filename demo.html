Simplicity is the goal, not the starting point.

With a background of fine arts and interaction design, Chih has close to two decades of experience designing software applications across platforms including web, television, social network, mobile, and desktop operating systems. Since the early 2000s, he has served as an art director, a producer, and a UX manager in small and large technology companies in New York City and the San Francisco Bay Area, and is currently leading the development of design systems and products that focus on cloud, mobility and multi-device user experiences. He is a gamer, a passionate music collector and a jazz pianist.
